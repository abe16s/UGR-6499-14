My Entertainments

When I get some free time or when I get bored or for whatever reason, I've some entertaining activities that got me covered. Starting from listening music in day to day activity to watching football games in the weekend, I have many entertainment alternatives that take my mind off the stressing and continuous school life and give me some relief.

Music

My first and foremost thing that I love to do is listen to music and get lost in the melody and rhythm. I listen to music in my all activities whether it is studying, walking or whatever, I enjoy music as much as I can because it gives a feeling of joy. Music also helps me get through tough times when I'm in bad mood, music never fails to cheer me up and its then when I found myself lost in the lyrics of songs. Music is just the soundtrack of my life. I enjoy all kinds of music but I like mostly PopRock, EDM, Ethiopian Reggae and Pop.

I listen to all kinds of songs & artists from OneRepublic, Kygo, Sia, Coldplay, Imagine Dragons and some Macklemore but here are some of my favorite artists and songs that I like very much.

Football

It wouldn't be exaggerating if I say I've been watching football since I know myself. I used to watch a lot of football growing up by going to DSTV houses and watching with a bunch of grown men. My father would take me there before the game starts and then again get me back at the end. And I have a lot of memories in those houses and football in general. I also like to play football myself with my friends at school breaks and during summer. As habit of a fan I have a team that I support from all the major European Leagues and from my country league. Here are some of them.

For me football means Manchester United. It runs through my blood and its something that stays with me till the end. Manchester United means everything to me I get very happy when it wins and very sad when it loses. From the good old days of watching Wayne Rooney (which by the way is my favorite player of all time, just love him), Paul Scholes, Ryan Giggs and the great Sir Alex Ferguson, I fell in love with the game because of man utd.

As a pursuit of supporting clubs from the major leagues, my Spanish counterpart is Barcelona. I fall in love with Barca's Tiki Taka with Messi, Inestia and Xavi in the middle of the pitch. Their ball was beautiful and unstoppable and it still is. They are one the few teams that play by holding the ball and playing an attractive game.

Of course there's got to be the German giants somewhere in here. Honestly I don't know how I got in to supporting Bayern Munich but I guess in the first years I started watching football most of the teams that I support now were the dominants in their respective leagues. And Bayern were the German ones.

Like most of my teams were the giants in the beginning of the 2010s, Inter Milan was no different. They won the trebel (Serie A, UEFA Champions League and the Coppa Italia) in 2009-10 under Jose Mourinho and players like Eto'o and sneijder.

Relax, I'm not just European football supporter, there is also one from my country Ethiopia. I am a Saint George (Kidus Giorgis) fan, yeah I'm a 'Feresegna'. I used to love Adane Girma very much when I was a kid and I loved how he played the game.

Movies

If I got long time to kill like during the summer or just quick lunch breaks I enjoy watching movies. I like the story movies tell and they way the make me immersed in them. I like mostly Comedy, Action, Adventure and Biographic movies. I enjoy single movies the most but also I like to see series movies here and there like especially sitcoms.

Here are the top 3 movies that I would see over and over again and still feel like it was the first time.

  • Grown Ups - 2013

    Five childhood friends take their families for a weekend trip to a lake house in their hometown, where they reunite to attend the funeral of their former high school basketball coach.

  • National Treasure - 2004

    Adventure/Action movie about a treasure hunter who is in hot pursuit of a mythical treasure that has been passed down for centuries, while his employer turned enemy is onto the same path that he is on.

  • Friends 1994-2004 (Tv-Series)

    Following the lives of six reckless adults living in Manhattan, as they indulge in adventures which make their lives both troublesome and happening.

Books

Last but not the least I also enjoy reading books whenever possible. Relative to the others I am lazy at reading books but still if I get the time I like reading books. What I like about is unlike movies, which form the images for the story in the video, while reading books I create my own characters, my own scenery and everything of the story is imagined and created in my mind. I like reading fictions the most like fantasies and adventures and also biographies which I learn from other successful people.
